Welcome to BinaryAge!

How much time do you spend working on a computer?
I'm a developer and I spend a lot of time in front of a computer screen. And that is why I really care about my software tools. I'm quite picky and I have strong opinions how my tools should work. Chances are you are similar to me. Let's share ideas, code and better workflows!

The story behind BinaryAge

I'm a former game programmer who morphed into a web developer. You can imagine it means pretty long way going from Windows, DirectX and proprietary C++ game engines to OSX, dynamic languages and open web technologies.

Why open-source?

I've been using many free and open-source tools during my career. I want to thank those authors and even if I didn't always contribute directly by donating money to them I really appreciate their work. This is sort of my pay-back. Working on cool projects is also a great opportunity to meet smart people and learn something.
