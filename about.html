Welcome to BinaryAge!

How much time do you spend working on a computer?

We are developers and we spend a lot of time in front of a computer screen. And that is why we really care about our software tools. We're quite picky and have strong opinions how tools should work. Chances are you are similar to us.

Let's share ideas, code and better workflows!

Need support?

If possible, we prefer you to post your issues to our public forums. Chances are that other people could help or maybe learn from our discussions.
