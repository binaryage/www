Welcome to BinaryAge!

How much time do you spend working on a computer?
I'm a developer and I spend a lot of time in front of a computer screen. And that is why I really care about my software tools. I'm quite picky and I have strong opinions how my tools should work. Chances are you are similar to me.
Let's share ideas, code and better workflows!

The story behind BinaryAge

I'm a former game programmer who morphed into a web developer. You can imagine it's a pretty long way going from Windows, DirectX and proprietary C++ game engines to OSX, dynamic languages and open web technologies.

Why open-source?

I made the tools primarily for myself because I wanted to work more effectively. Releasing them open-source was just a logical step and an opportunity to contribute back to the community.
