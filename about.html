Welcome to Binary Age!

How much time do you spend working on a computer? Daily? Monthly? How much every year?
And how do you feel about it?

Listen, I'm a programmer and I spend a lot of time in front of the computer screen.
And that is why I really care about my software tools. I'm quite picky and I have strong opinions.

I have been always looking for ways how to work smarter. How to make things better in the long run. It's about productivity. It's about fun. It's about having a good feeling about the way I work.

Chances are you are similar to me. Chances are you have the same needs. Chances are you live in the binary age as well as me. Let's share ideas, code and better workflows.

Join thousands of developers who use BinaryAge tools.

Contributions

Some of my friends don't understand why I'm doing this. Why I give something away for free. Why I'm not working on another client project instead and making *real money*.

First, I've been using many free and open-source tools during my career. I want to thank those authors and even if I didn't always contribute directly by donating them money I really appreciate their work. This is sort of my pay-back.

Second, I simply love the idea that something I've built primarily for myself and which helps me in my daily work is directly useful for other people working like me. Tweet!

Third, I hope my work will be recognized and I will possibly get opportunity to work on interesting projects and ideas in the future. Have a gig? Let's talk!

Anyway, if you want to contribute, I prefer patches on GitHub, but here is also paypal button. This is nothing I can make living of but I really appreciate your donations same as hearing "thank you".
